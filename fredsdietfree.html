Refeeds are your secret weapon against the metabolic slowdown caused by calorie restriction. By strategically increasing calories, especially from carbohydrates, we can boost leptin levels, reinvigorate your metabolism, and support muscle growth.

Refeed Benefits

  • Leptin level optimization
  • Enhanced anabolic environment

Refeed Blueprint

  • Frequency: Twice weekly (e.g., Monday and Friday)
  • Calorie target: 16 calories per pound of bodyweight
  • Macronutrient split:
    • Protein: 30% of total calories
    • Fat: 25% of total calories
    • Carbs: 45% of total calories
