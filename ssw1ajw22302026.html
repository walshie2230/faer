SAIYAN SAGA L1 (PRE-PROGRAM) - Workout Sheet

WEEK 1

Workout 1

Skill Training: Negative Chin-Ups

Max reps for 100sec

Workout Routine:

  • 5 Pull-ups
  • 10 Tricep Dips
  • 10 Push-ups

5 sets with 1min break in between

Workout 2

Skill Training: Push-Ups

Max reps for 100sec

Workout Routine:

Each exercise for 30sec:

  • Burpees
  • Plank
  • Squat Calf Raises
  • Push-ups
  • Lunges
  • Side Plank (right)
  • Side Plank (left)

4 sets with 1min break

Workout 3

Skill Training: Bodyweight Rows

Max reps for 100sec

Workout Routine:

  • 20 Push-ups
  • 60sec Plank
  • 30sec Side Plank (right)
  • 30sec Side Plank (left)
  • 30 Squat Calf Raises
  • 60sec Leg Flutters

4 sets with 1min break in between

Workout 4

Skill Training: Burpees

Max reps for 100sec

Workout Routine:

Each exercise for 1min:

  • Burpees
  • Leg Flutters
  • Lunges
  • Glute Bridge
  • Squat Jumps
  • Plank

3 sets with 1min break in between
